Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the tracker?

Simply copy the Javascript snippet from your dashboard and paste it into the <head> section of your website code.

Does it track unique visitors?

Yes, we use cookies and IP fingerprinting to distinguish between returning users and new unique visitors.

Is this free?

We offer a free tier for up to 10,000 monthly page views. Pro plans start at $10/mo.
